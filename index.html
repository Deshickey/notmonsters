BIO

Not Monsters is what happens when you smash the melodic experimentalism of CFIT together with the powerhouse riffing of Punch Face Champions.

Despite only joining forces a year ago, this supergroup have already played a number of shows around Ireland, beginning with Vantastival, and including Canalphonic, State’s Faces 2016, Hard Working Class Heroes, Electric Picnic and Knockanstockan.


“a formidable meeting of sheer power, melody and smart songwriting,” - State Magazine
